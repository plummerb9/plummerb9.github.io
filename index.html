Skils'kin Employee Handbook

Introduction

This employee handbook provides general information about Skils'kin and its policies and procedures pertaining to employment. The information in the Skils'kin Employee Handbook was compiled for employees to use as a guide regarding employment decisions and expectations. Every employment situation cannot be addressed in the Skils'kin Employee Handbook, and employment decisions will be determined based on pertinent circumstances in addition to the guidelines in the Skils'kin Employee Handbook.

The contents of the Skils'kin Employee Handbook do not create a contract of employment for any specific duration. Except for the CEO, no one at Skils'kin has the authority to enter into a contractual employment agreement for a specified duration. Additionally, only the CEO may alter or modify the policies in this handbook. Statements or promises by the CEO or any Skils'kin representative will not be interpreted as an official change in policy nor will it constitute and employment agreement.

Employees must familiarize themselves with the contents of the Skils'kin Employee Handbook. For additional guidance on policies related to employment at Skils'kin, employees can contact Human Resources.
